Down to Earth

I was lucky enough to be given the opportunity to blog for the University of Florida's college of sustainability studies in the spring of 2013. The blog is updated weekly when school is in session, and covers a variety of topics relating to sustainability.

I chose the title "Down to Earth" because I think it sums up my premise neatly. I want to create something readable and engaging, that promotes sustainability in a simple and relevant way. My hope is to entice people who normally wouldn't consider "going green" a priority, and to offer them insight into the possibilities of a more sustainable lifestyle.
