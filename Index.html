Adventurous Alex

The ongoing story of a 20-year-old aspiring environmental journalist at the University of Florida.

I didn't always know I wanted to be a journalist, but sometimes I feel like I'm made for it. Reading and writing have been my passions since the day I learned them, but the communication aspect of journalism came a bit later.

Story about my life. voracious.

Day one of my Intro to Journalism course at the University of Florida felt like I'd come home. Everything the professor said spoke to me. Do I like to read and write all the time? Check. Do I love talking to strangers about (at times) intensely personal issues? Check. Do I like learning a little bit about everything under the sun? Check, check, check.
